How to Hack the Vigenere Cipher

Examine the ciphertext for any and all repeating letter patterns

Count the distance (number of letters) from start of sequence to start of sequence

The Vigenere Key Length will be a COMMON FACTOR of all pattern’s spacing lengths

Once you’ve decided on a possible key, perform a frequency analysis of every nth letter

Match expected frequencies to observed frequencies to determine how the alphabet was
shifted – this will give you the first letter of the KeyWord

Do the same frequency comparisons of every kth letter of the KeyWord until you know the Key

Once you know the key: it’s time to get your hack on!
